St. Anne Institute is a private, not for profit, secular residential and community based preventive service agency committed to providing the highest level of care and rehabilitation services in the briefest time to children and families throughout New York State. We are located on an eight and one half-acre campus in the heart of Albany, New York. Each year, thousands of children, young adults and families benefit from St. Anne Institute’s programs and services. Admissions Residential Services St. Anne Institute’s residential program serves young women and their families from throughout New York State Community Services Open to both male & female clients and their families (except the Day Services Educational Program which is open only to adolescent girls ages 12-21 in the capital region) St. Anne’s non-residential programs share the common philosophy that families are the focus of our interventions. By emphasizing family strengths and by helping children and their family’s cope with the extreme stresses they confront, we help families break cycles of abuse and victimization.
