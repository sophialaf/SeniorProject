Our Mission:

The Eden's Rose Foundation is an international 501c3 non-profit
whos mission is to relieve the suffering of the most impoverished
and marginalized children in the world. Our work to end the cycle
of poverty through community-wide empowerment and sustainability
projects is rooted in the belief that all children need and deserve
the basic provisions of access to economic opportunity, nutritious food,
financial education, and a safe sanctuary.

Our goal is not only to survive but to thrive, to live the highest
reaches of the human experience as we learn and grow together.
Each of us plays a unique and necessary part as our hearts guide us,
inciting immediate and lasting social justice, access to basic needs,
and environmental, economic, and cultural sustainability.
